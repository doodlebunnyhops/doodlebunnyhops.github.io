**Signal Array Online/Disruption**

Part of [Chair Room](#_ox7f1mpoe8n0) quest

**Steps:**

1. Go inside of the Agency Bunker
2. Turn off light switch: Message reads: **Signal Array Disruption**

	1. Deactivates A08
